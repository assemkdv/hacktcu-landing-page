About the event

HackTCU is a high-energy hackathon where students build, innovate, and bring ideas to life. Work in teams, learn new skills, and compete to create something amazing in just 24 hours. Whether you're new or experienced, this is your chance to code, collaborate, and stand out.

What to Expect

  • Teamwork: Collaborate with other students
  • Learning: Gain new technical skills
  • Competition: Build something amazing
